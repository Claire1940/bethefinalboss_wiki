Once you have mastered the basics of **be the final boss souls farming**, you need to look toward the endgame. Endless mode is where the real Soul wealth is hidden, but it requires a strategic shift from "damage" to "sustainability."

### The Breakthrough Recovery Strategy
If a group of heroes breaks through your minion line, do not panic and abandon the front. Use your character's mobility to dash back, clear the immediate threat to the castle, and then return to the front line. If you stay at the castle, you stop farming the heroes still at the entrance, which kills your Soul-per-minute efficiency.

| Survival Tactic | Description | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chokepoint Stacking** | Placing all minions in one spot | Concentrated damage |
| **Kiting** | Leading heroes away from the gate | Buys time for cooldowns |
| **Priority Targeting** | Killing fast heroes first | Prevents cheap losses |
