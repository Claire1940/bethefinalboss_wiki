Your weapon does not exist in a vacuum. To truly master the **be the final boss weapon tier list**, you must understand how your personal damage complements your summoned minions. A high-damage weapon allows you to act as a "cleaner," picking off the heroes that your minions have already weakened.

**Minion Roles vs. Weapon Support:**
- **Tank Minions**: Hold heroes in place, allowing you to use weapons with slower, high-damage attack patterns safely.
- **DPS Minions**: Soften up groups, letting you finish them off with quick, low-damage strikes.
- **Ranged Minions**: Provide cover fire while you engage heroes at the frontline.
