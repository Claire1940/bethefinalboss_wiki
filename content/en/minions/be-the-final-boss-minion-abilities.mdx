<Section icon={Shield} title="Detailed Ability Breakdown: Melee vs. Ranged">

The distinction between melee and ranged **be the final boss minion abilities** defines your tactical approach. Melee minions possess abilities centered around "Threat" and "Stall," whereas ranged minions focus on "Execution" and "Uptime."

### Melee Abilities (The Frontline)
Melee minions are your primary shield. Their abilities often include high-impact strikes that can momentarily stun or slow heroes, giving you more time to react. In the Skill Tree, focusing on minion health and defense directly buffs these units, allowing them to hold the line longer.

### Ranged Abilities (The Backline)
Ranged units provide the necessary damage to actually clear the waves. Their abilities often have longer cooldowns but much higher damage modifiers. Because they do not need to move as much as melee units, their "Damage Per Second" (DPS) is usually much more consistent.

Mastering **be the final boss minion abilities** requires more than just summoning; it requires positioning and synergy. As the waves scale, heroes will begin to use their own abilities, such as heals or shields, which you must counter with your minion composition.

### Synergy Tactics
- **The Choke Point**: Position your melee minions at narrow entryways. This forces heroes to bunch up, making them vulnerable to your caster's AoE abilities.
