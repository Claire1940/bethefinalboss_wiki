<Section icon={Shield} title="Minion Management and Defensive Lineups">

Minions are the backbone of your defense in Be The Final Boss. While your character is powerful, you cannot be everywhere at once. A well-structured army prevents heroes from reaching the castle gates, which is the primary failure condition in any trial. Minions are purchased and upgraded using Souls, a currency earned specifically by defeating hero waves.

Understanding the hierarchy of units is essential for long-term survival. Not all minions serve the same purpose; some are designed to soak up damage, while others provide high-DPS support from the backline.

| Minion Tier | Type | Strengths | Best Placement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **S-Tier** | Elite Boss Minions | Massive HP, Area-of-Effect (AoE) damage | Front and center of the main path |
| **A-Tier** | Durable Defenders | High knockback, Moderate health | Intersections and chokepoints |
| **B-Tier** | Ranged Casters | High single-target damage, Slow effects | Behind melee units or on elevated platforms |
| **C-Tier** | Starter Melee | Low cost, Fast respawn | Used as distraction fodder for early heroes |

<Callout type="warning" title="Defensive Warning">
Avoid clustering all your minions in a single spot. Certain hero types in later waves have AoE attacks that can wipe out your entire defensive line if they are standing too close together.
</Callout>

When building your army, prioritize a "Meat Shield" approach. Place your most durable units directly in the path of the heroes to halt their momentum. This gives you, the Final Boss, the opportunity to use your high-damage skills without being targeted by multiple enemies simultaneously.
