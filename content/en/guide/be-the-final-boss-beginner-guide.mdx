<Section icon={Target} title="Building Your First Dungeon Floors">

Your dungeon layout is the most critical factor in your survival. A well-designed floor forces heroes to waste their resources (healing potions and mana) before they even reach your elite guards. Follow these steps to set up a basic but effective defensive perimeter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Entrance Chokepoint">
    Design the first room to be a "Drain Room." Use low-cost traps like **Spike Pits** or **Slow Slime** to force heroes to use their movement abilities early.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Minion Synergy Rooms">
    Place a barracks room next. Ensure the minions here have **Elemental Synergy**. For example, use a Fire Mage minion behind a line of Shield Guards to maximize damage while staying protected.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The False Treasure Room">
    Create a room filled with "Mimics" or "Ambush Traps." Heroes are programmed to prioritize treasure chests; use this greed against them to split their party.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Boss Antechamber">
    This is the final room before the Core. Place your **Elite Guard** here. These units should have high crowd control (CC) abilities to stun the hero party's healer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Linear Paths">
Never build a straight line to your core. Heroes gain "Momentum" when moving quickly through rooms. Use **L-shaped corridors** to break their line of sight and force them into traps.
</Callout>

</Section>
